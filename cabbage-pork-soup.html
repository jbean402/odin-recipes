Cabbage and Pork Soup

Description

Inspired by the French dessert, the Mille Feuille Nabe is a savory rendition. It is a soup with layered cabbage and thin pork slices covered with a broth. Many also add mushrooms and ginger slices into the mix to make it more fragrant.

Steps

  1. Start preparing the broth by combining the dashi powder, soy sauce, mirin, and sesame oil - preferrably by personal taste. Let the mixture simmer with water.
  2. Layer the slices of pork and cabbage. Do not go over 10 layers per set.
  3. Once done, start to slice the napa cabbage into thick slices.
  4. Start layering the slices of cabbage and pork into the pot. Do this in a flower like shape and until all the cabbage is gone.
  5. Pour the broth over the slices. Add mushrooms and other toppings as well.
  6. Enjoy!
